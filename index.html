about us

UAV GLOBAL INTEGRATED CONCEPT LIMITED

UAV GLOBAL INTEGRATED CONCEPT LIMITED was established and registered as a Limited Liability Company on 6th February, 2017. Our company has grown from modest beginning into a premier procurement construction organisation operating in the International market, supplying all sectors of economy as well as oil and gas industries, which have become our major focus. As a result of this, the company has evolved into major supplies of electrical equipment to the oil and gas, petrochemical industries especially the Shell Petroleum Development Company Limited (SPDC) J Ifeanyi Nigeria Limited, Kurus Nigeria Limited, Etc.
